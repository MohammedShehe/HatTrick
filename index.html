Our story

Bringing Global Beauty Trends to Tanzania

Founded by Omar Khalid Said, Hat-Trick Scents started with a vision to fuse Zanzibar’s aromatic heritage with world-class perfumery and cosmetics.

We source ethical, cruelty-free brands and blend them with local warmth. Located in the heart of Stone Town at Michenzani Mall — we serve both walk-in clients and nationwide delivery. Our promise: authenticity, elegance, and unmatched customer care.
